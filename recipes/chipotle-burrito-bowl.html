Chipotle Burrito Bowl

Description:

Chipotle style burrito bowl with a bed of grains, sizable protein portions of ground beef, veggies, sour cream topping, and additionally garnishes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. ground beef, 80/20 or 93/7 ratio; meat/fat
  • 1 whole yellow onion
  • Sour cream, amount is preference
