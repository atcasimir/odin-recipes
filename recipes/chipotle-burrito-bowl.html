Chipotle Burrito Bowl

Description:

Chipotle style burrito bowl with a bed of grains, sizable protein portions of ground beef, veggies, sour cream topping, and additionally garnishes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. ground beef, 80/20 or 93/7 ratio; meat/fat
  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1 whole yellow onion
  • Sour cream, amount is preference
  • 1 tbsp. garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp. onion powder
  • 1/2 tbsp. paprika
  • 1/2 tbsp. salt
  • 1/2 tbsp. pepper
  • Ready fresh (no cooking required) greens of choice, e.g. lettuce, cucumber, spinach, etc.

Steps:

  1. First begin by placing the rinsed rice into a crock pot with 1 and 3/4 cup water. Select “rice/grain”, and then “Enter”.
  2. Now chop up the onion into with the grain slices, so you end up with long slices of the onions natural structure.
  3. Heat up 1 fairly large for the beef and 1 medium sized for the onion with oil.
  4. Transfer the onion to the oiled pan and set on medium and cover with a lid.
  5. Place the beef into the larger pan and begin smashing with a large plastic/wood spoon to separate the meat into smaller pieces
  6. Periodically check on the onion to ensure it isn't burning or getting too cooked, ideally a nice golden/yellow color is perfect, potentially a little browning if preferred.
  7. After the beef has cooked for a few minutes, add in the spices and stir well. The beef will need more time to continue cooking off the fat.
  8. The onions should be close to finished if their colored has reached the golden yellow established previously, if they are at this point, remove the pan from the heat.
  9. Continue cooking the beef until there is no more pink meat present, and a nice browning has formed on a majority of the beef.
