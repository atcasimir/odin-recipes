Italian Sausage and Pasta

Description:

This is one of my favorite meals! It is my go to whenever I can’t decide on what to cook. It is a simple buttered noodles and hot italian sausage meal, topped with freshly grated genuine parmesan.

Ingredients:

  • 3-5 Hot Italian Sausages from Kroger
  • 1 box DeCecco no. 34 spiral noodles
  • 1 tsp. olive oil
  • 1 tsp. salted/unsalted butter
  • Genuine Parmesan wedge

Steps:

  1. Begin with filling a pot with water and salting to taste, and place on burner to begin boiling.
  2. While the water is boiling, place the italian sausage in a pan on medium-high heat.
  3. Let the sausage brown and form a good crust no both sides, and then slightly brown the inner and outer-curved side of the sausage link if the water is still not boiling yet.
  4. Once the water is boiling, throw in pasta and turn down the heat so the water doesn't boil over the edge. The pasta should finish cooking in roughly 10-13 minutes
  5. With the pasta cooking, put about a half-cup of water in the pan with the sausage to begin steaming. Turn the heat up to max and place a lid on to trap the steam.
  6. Occasionally stir the pasta to keep the foam from boiling over, and check the sausage periodically.
  7. When the water in the sausage pan boils down, pour in another half-cup to continue steaming. The pasta should be about half done by now.
  8. Continue to check both pasta and sausage, when the second steaming is done (all the water is boiled away), the pasta should be finished as well.
  9. With the pasta complete, drain it through a strainer, and turn off all of the burners.
  10. Plate the pasta in a bowl, and the sausage on a plate. From here, put a teaspoon of oil, butter, and salt into the pasta, then shred some fresh parmesan onto the pasta and sausages.

Voila, your meal is complete!
